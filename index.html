Lesson 4.0 Overview

What you'll study in this lesson

📚 Key Themes

  • ▸Women's status before and after the Song Dynasty
  • ▸The practice of foot-binding (缠足) and its social effects
  • ▸The May Fourth Movement (五四运动) and cultural change
  • ▸Women's rights after 1949 and the founding of the PRC
  • ▸Progress and remaining challenges in gender equality
  • ▸三从四德 and the Confucian code for women

🔡 Vocabulary Focus

  • ▸女字旁 — 妇、媳、嫁、婆、姻… (women, kinship, marriage)
  • ▸米字旁 — 糟 in 糟糕 (character family anchor)
  • ▸木字旁 + 权 — 夫权、权利、权益 (power and rights)
  • ▸Easily confused pairs: 享有 vs 享受, 甚至 vs 即使
  • ▸Formal writing words: 至此, 高于, 目前, 总之
  • ▸56 new words total in the vocabulary table
